Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Data Collection

CloudSlash is a local CLI tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit your AWS credentials or infrastructure data. All scanning happens locally on your machine.

2. License Verification

When you use a license key, the software performs a local check. No data is sent to our servers.

3. Contact Information

If you contact us for support, we will store your email address solely for the purpose of communicating with you.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
